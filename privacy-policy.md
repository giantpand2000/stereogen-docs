# Privacy Policy — StereoGen

**Last updated:** 2026-07-15
**Publisher:** Lan Fan
**Application:** StereoGen (`com.lanfan.stereogen`)

This page is hosted as the public privacy statement for StereoGen. It is not
legal advice; have counsel review it if your circumstances require legal review.

## Overview

StereoGen is a desktop application that creates single-image stereograms
(autostereograms) from a **depth map** and a **texture** that you provide.
Core processing runs **entirely on your device**. StereoGen does not require an
account and does not upload your images to servers operated by Lan Fan.

## Data we process

| Data | How it is used | Leaves your device? |
|------|----------------|---------------------|
| Depth map and texture files you open | Read locally to generate a stereogram | No (unless you save/share the result yourself) |
| Generated stereogram | Shown in the app; saved only if you choose Save | No |
| Generation options (algorithm, parallax, etc.) | Remembered for convenience | No — stored only in the app config directory on your PC |

### Local configuration storage

On Windows, option preferences may be stored under a path similar to:

`%APPDATA%\com.lanfan.stereogen\`

(Exact location is determined by the OS and the app identifier.)

You can delete this folder to reset preferences. Uninstalling the app may or may
not remove it, depending on the installer and Windows settings.

## Data we do not collect

Lan Fan does **not**, through the StereoGen application:

- Create user accounts or require sign-in  
- Collect names, email addresses, or payment data inside the app  
- Upload depth maps, textures, or generated images to our servers  
- Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the app (as of the date above)  
- Track your location  

## Network use

Generating stereograms does **not** require network access.

The Windows installer may install or update the **Microsoft WebView2** runtime
(used to display the app UI). That component is provided by Microsoft and is
subject to Microsoft’s terms and privacy policy.

If you download StereoGen from the Microsoft Store or our website, that
download and any store account activity are governed by **Microsoft** (or your
host’s) policies, not by this document alone.

## Children

StereoGen is a general-purpose graphics utility. It is not directed at children
under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your choices

- Do not open files you do not want the app to read.  
- Save outputs only to locations you control.  
- Clear or delete the local config directory to remove stored options.  
- Uninstall the application via Windows Settings or the installer.
